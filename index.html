FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you might want to know before your first visit to SmileLab.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes — we work with most major insurance providers and handle the paperwork for you. We also offer flexible in-house payment plans for treatments not fully covered.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Absolutely. Our professional-grade whitening is supervised by a dentist and is far safer and more effective than over-the-counter kits, with little to no sensitivity.

How often should I get a checkup?

For most patients we recommend a cleaning and exam every six months. We'll tailor a schedule to your needs after your first visit.

Will my treatment hurt?

Comfort is our priority. We use gentle techniques and modern anesthesia, and most patients are surprised by how pain-free their visit is.

Do you see children?

Yes — our pediatric care is gentle and kid-friendly, designed to make young patients feel relaxed and build healthy habits early.

What if I have a dental emergency?

Call us right away — we reserve same-day slots for emergencies like severe pain, broken teeth, or trauma, so you're never left waiting in pain.
